The Gibson Explorer

The Explorer is one of the coolest guitars ever made. It came out in 1958 and it still looks like nothing else out there.

"There's no mistaking an Explorer on stage."

Why it's good

It's got a weird pointy shape but it actually sits really well on a strap because of the long bottom horn.
It's mostly mahogany which gives it a heavy sound with a lot of sustain.
The scale length is longer than a Les Paul so the low strings sound tighter and deeper.
It uses two humbuckers so it's simple to plug in and get a big rock tone with no fuss.
Everyone recognizes the shape instantly, even people who don't play guitar.

Famous people who play one

  • The Edge from U2
  • James Hetfield from Metallica
  • Allen Collins from Lynyrd Skynyrd
  • Rick Nielsen from Cheap Trick
  • Eric Clapton

Basically if you want a guitar that looks different from everyone else's and still sounds huge, the Explorer is one of the best options out there.
