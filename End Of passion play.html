Third Studio Album · 1986

METALLICA

Master of Puppets

Released Mar 3, 1986 Elektra Records Sweet Silence Studios, Copenhagen
About the Record

Recorded in Copenhagen with producer Flemming Rasmussen, this is the record that turned Metallica from an underground thrash act into a genre-defining force, and it remains the last album to feature bassist Cliff Burton.

Clocking in at under an hour across eight tracks, it trades hooks for structure — long-form arrangements, tempo shifts, and riffs built to be played live for decades.
