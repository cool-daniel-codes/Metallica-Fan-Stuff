72 Seasons

Released Apr 14, 2023 Blackened Recordings HQ, San Rafael, California
About the Record

Produced by Greg Fidelman alongside Hetfield and Ulrich, 72 Seasons explores the concept of the first 18 years of our lives that form our true or false selves. It stands as a heavy, driving modern record that looks backward at foundational influences while moving forward.

Clocking in at over 77 minutes, it delivers a massive, relentless wall of riffs, pairing deeply personal lyrical themes of reckoning and light with the band's core thrash and heavy metal roots.
